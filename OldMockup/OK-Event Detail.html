About the Event

Sensuality & Essentiality is much more than a boy/girl stylo event - it's a transformative experience designed to take your dance to another level. Here you will not only perfect your technique, but you will face the most important challenge: stepping out of your comfort zone.

Through intensive workshops, personal exploration dynamics and conscious training, you will immerse yourself in a journey where projection and essence become key tools to unlock your authentic style. You will learn to master your energy, express yourself with confidence and find your own voice in movement, whether you are a boy, a girl or exploring both languages.

This is the space where you break boundaries, awaken your body and free your mind. Because when you connect with your essence, your stylo becomes your superpower.

Welcome to Sensuality & Essentiality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person up to 7 days before the event. Please contact support for assistance.

Are there parking facilities at the venue?

Yes, Fira Gran Via has ample underground parking available. The daily rate is €20. We recommend using public transport as it is very convenient.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not permitted inside the venue. There will be a variety of food trucks and stalls available on-site.
